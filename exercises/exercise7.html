MS Word Manual for Open Education Librarians

Discover practical tips and techniques for creating accessible documents with my manual, "An Open Education Librarian's Guide to Microsoft Word 2024." This guide offers easy-to-follow instructions on how to enhance document accessibility through styles, tables of contents, hyperlinks, alternative text, and sharing options. Perfect for educators, librarians, and anyone committed to creating open, accessible resources for all users.

Grant Proposal for students in Nucchu, Bolivia

My grant proposal, submitted to The Global Partnership for Education, seeked funding to provide 20 new HP laptops to the U.E. German Busch Primary School in Nucchu, Bolivia. With limited access to educational resources, internet, and finances, the students in this rural school face significant barriers to learning. By partnering with the World Librarians program, which has previously supported schools in Malawi and Kenya, this project aims to provide the children of Nucchu with essential technology to access educational resources and bridge the gap to a brighter future.

Community Over Commercialization Panel

The panel, Community Over Commercialization, held at the W.E.B. Du Bois Library on October 24, 2023, celebrated Open Access Week and highlighted the UMass World Librarians program. The six-member panel shared the program's mission to connect underserved communities in Malawi and Kenya to online open educational resources. The panel emphasized the importance of community-driven initiatives, highlighting the program's commitment to overcoming technological disparities and promoting open access to knowledge.

Final Photography Project-Hands in Barcelona

For our final assignment in Photography in Barcelona we had to choose a concept and produce 10-12 photos expressing that concept. We had free range on what concept we wanted to explore and were encouraged to try something outside of our comfort zone. I chose to focus on hands and the human connection in Barcelona. These were the 12 images I felt depicted this theme.
